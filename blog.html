DOS MUCHOS BASEBALL

DosMuchos Baseball is a passion project I started two summers ago as a creative outlet. I had no webdev or journalism education really, I just really liked baseball and always enjoyed writing when I was in school. So, I bought a domain name and headed to wordpress. I was soon overwhelmed with html and the coding necessary to make something look nice with wordpress. I would always spend more time trying to figure out why my site didn't look the way I wanted and less time watching and writing about baseball. Soon my frustration got the better of me and I stopped writing and posting; but with my newly aquired skills I hope to revive my blog this summer!
